About the Department

The Department of Cyber Security at Bangalore Institute of Technology is dedicated to producing world-class security professionals who can protect digital assets and combat cyber threats. Established in 2020, our department focuses on the critical domain of information security, ethical hacking, digital forensics, and cyber defense strategies.

With cyber attacks becoming increasingly sophisticated, our program prepares students to become the first line of defense for organizations worldwide. Our curriculum combines theoretical foundations with hands-on training in state-of-the-art security labs, ensuring graduates are ready to tackle real-world security challenges.

Vision

To be a premier center for cyber security education and research, producing ethical security professionals who safeguard the digital world and contribute to national cyber security initiatives.

Mission

To provide comprehensive security education through hands-on training, foster research in emerging security domains, and develop professionals with strong ethical foundations to protect digital infrastructure.

📚 Programs Offered

B.Tech in Cyber Security

Duration: 4 Years | Intake: 60 Seats

Comprehensive program covering network security, cryptography, ethical hacking, digital forensics, and security operations.

M.Tech in Cyber Security

Duration: 2 Years | Intake: 30 Seats

Advanced program focusing on security analytics, malware analysis, cloud security, and security architecture.

PG Diploma in Information Security

Duration: 1 Year | Intake: 30 Seats

Industry-focused program for professionals seeking expertise in security management and compliance.

Year 1 - Foundation

  • Introduction to Cyber Security
  • Programming for Security (Python, C)
  • Computer Networks & Protocols
  • Cryptography Fundamentals

Year 2 - Core Security

  • Network Security & Firewalls
  • Ethical Hacking & Penetration Testing
  • Web Application Security
  • Operating System Security

Year 3 - Advanced Topics

  • Digital Forensics & Incident Response
  • Malware Analysis & Reverse Engineering
  • Cloud & IoT Security
  • Security Operations Center (SOC)

Year 4 - Specialization & Project

  • Threat Intelligence & Hunting
  • Security Governance & Compliance
  • Capstone Security Project
  • Industry Internship
