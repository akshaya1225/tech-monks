Department of Computer Science & Engineering

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering is dedicated to producing skilled professionals who can lead the digital revolution. With state-of-the-art labs and industry-aligned curriculum, we prepare students for successful careers in technology.

B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering

📅 Duration: 4 Years | 🎓 Intake: 120 Seats

🎯 Program Objectives:

  • Build strong foundation in programming, algorithms, and data structures
  • Develop expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science
  • Prepare students for global IT industry and research opportunities
  • Foster innovation and entrepreneurial mindset

📖 Core Subjects:

  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Cloud Computing & DevOps

💼 Career Opportunities:

  • Software Engineer | Data Scientist | AI/ML Engineer
  • Cloud Architect | Cybersecurity Analyst | Full Stack Developer

M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering

📅 Duration: 2 Years | 🎓 Intake: 30 Seats

🎯 Program Objectives:

  • Advanced specialization in cutting-edge technologies
  • Research-oriented curriculum with thesis component
  • Industry collaboration for real-world problem solving
  • Prepare for leadership roles in R&D and academia

📖 Specializations:

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Data Science & Big Data Analytics
  • Cyber Security & Blockchain

Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering focuses on cutting-edge research in VLSI, Embedded Systems, and Communication Technologies. Our curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on lab experience.

B.Tech in Electronics & Communication Engineering

📅 Duration: 4 Years | 🎓 Intake: 90 Seats

🎯 Program Objectives:

  • Master core concepts in electronics, communication systems, and signal processing
  • Develop expertise in VLSI design, embedded systems, and IoT
  • Prepare students for careers in semiconductor, telecom, and consumer electronics

📖 Core Subjects:

  • Analog & Digital Electronics
  • Communication Systems
  • VLSI Design & Fabrication
  • Embedded Systems & IoT
  • Signal Processing & Antenna Design

Department of Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering focuses on design, manufacturing, thermal engineering, and robotics. Our students gain hands-on experience through industry projects and state-of-the-art labs.

B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering

📅 Duration: 4 Years | 🎓 Intake: 90 Seats

🎯 Program Objectives:

  • Build strong foundation in design, thermodynamics, and manufacturing
  • Develop expertise in CAD/CAM, robotics, and automation
  • Prepare students for automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing sectors

📖 Core Subjects:

  • Thermodynamics & Heat Transfer
  • Machine Design & Manufacturing Processes
  • CAD/CAM & Robotics
  • Automotive Engineering
  • Renewable Energy Systems
