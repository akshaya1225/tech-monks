## 📖 Overview

A modern, responsive institutional website for Bangalore Institute of Technology (BIT), featuring a cyberpunk-inspired design with dark/light theme support. The website showcases the institute's facilities, programs, placements, and campus life with interactive elements including an AI-powered chatbot and dynamic content sections.

## ✨ Features

### 🎨 Design & Theme
- **Cyberpunk Aesthetic** - Modern design with neon accents and futuristic elements
- **Dark/Light Theme Toggle** - User preference saved in localStorage
- **Responsive Layout** - Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Animated Background** - Dynamic grid and floating orb effects in dark mode

### 📱 Navigation
- Sticky navigation bar with smooth hover effects
- Mobile-responsive hamburger menu
- Active page highlighting
- Quick access to all major sections

### 🏠 Home Page Features
- **Hero Slider** - Auto-rotating image carousel with manual controls
- **Auto-Sliding Image Carousel** - 10+ campus images scrolling seamlessly left
- **About Section** - Key highlights of the institute
- **Discover BIT Section** - Interactive cards showcasing:
  - Student Housing
  - Sports & Recreation
  - Cultural Activities
  - Green Campus
  - Research Centers
  - Publications
  - Innovation Lab
  - Startup Incubator
  - Patents & IP
  - Industry Partnerships

### 📡 Technical News Section
- **Dynamic News Updates** - Latest technology and research news from the institute
- **Image Collage** - Visual representation of campus events and achievements
- **Real-time Updates** - News highlights including:
  - National Robotics Championship wins
  - AI Research Lab inauguration with NVIDIA partnership
  - Patent filings for sustainable energy solutions
  - ISRO collaboration for space technology research
- **Explore More** - Link to detailed technical news page

### 🤖 AI Chatbot Assistant
**"BIT Assistant" - Intelligent Virtual Helper**

The chatbot provides instant responses to common queries:

#### Capabilities:
- **Admissions** - Process, entrance exams (KCET, COMEDK, JEE), eligibility criteria
- **Placements** - Highest packages, top recruiters (Google, Microsoft, Amazon), placement statistics
- **Fee Structure** - B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA fees, scholarships information
- **Hostel Facilities** - Accommodation details, mess facilities, amenities
- **Scholarships** - Merit-based, government schemes, private scholarships
- **Campus Facilities** - Library, labs, sports, medical center
- **Programs Offered** - B.Tech specializations, M.Tech courses, MBA, PhD
- **Important Dates** - Admission timeline, academic calendar
- **Contact Information** - Phone numbers, email addresses, campus address

#### Interactive Features:
- **Quick Question Buttons** - One-click access to common topics
- **Typing Animation** - Realistic bot response simulation
- **Message History** - Full conversation tracking
- **Responsive Design** - Works on all devices
- **Theme Integration** - Adapts to dark/light mode
