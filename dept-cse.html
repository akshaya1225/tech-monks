About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Bangalore Institute of Technology was established in 1979 with a vision to produce world-class computer professionals. The department has consistently been ranked among the top computer science departments in Karnataka.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, we prepare students for successful careers in software development, research, and entrepreneurship. Our curriculum is regularly updated to keep pace with the rapidly evolving technology landscape.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in computer science education and research, producing innovative technologists who contribute to the global digital economy.

Mission

To provide quality education through industry-aligned curriculum, foster research and innovation, and develop ethical professionals with strong problem-solving skills.

📚 Programs Offered

B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Intake: 120 Seats

Comprehensive program covering algorithms, data structures, software engineering, AI, and more.

M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering

Duration: 2 Years | Intake: 60 Seats

Advanced program with specializations in AI, Cloud Computing, and Data Science.

Ph.D in Computer Science & Engineering

Duration: 3-5 Years | Intake: 20 Seats

Research program with focus on cutting-edge areas like AI, Blockchain, and Quantum Computing.
