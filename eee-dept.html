About the Department

The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering at Bangalore Institute of Technology was established in 1979 with a vision to produce world-class electrical engineers. The department has consistently been ranked among the top electrical engineering departments in Karnataka.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, we prepare students for successful careers in power systems, electronics design, automation, and renewable energy. Our curriculum is regularly updated to keep pace with the rapidly evolving technology landscape.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in electrical and electronics engineering education and research, producing innovative engineers who contribute to sustainable energy solutions and advanced electronics.

Mission

To provide quality education through industry-aligned curriculum, foster research in power electronics and automation, and develop ethical professionals with strong problem-solving skills.

📚 Programs Offered

B.Tech in Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Intake: 90 Seats

Comprehensive program covering power systems, control systems, electronics, and renewable energy.

B.Tech in Electronics & Communication Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Intake: 120 Seats

Advanced program focusing on VLSI design, embedded systems, and communication technologies.

M.Tech in Power Electronics

Duration: 2 Years | Intake: 30 Seats

Specialized program in power electronics, renewable energy systems, and smart grids.

🔬 Laboratories & Facilities

Power Systems Lab

High voltage testing facility with transformers, transmission line models, and protection relays.

Power Electronics Lab

Complete setup for converters, inverters, and motor drives with DSP controllers.

Renewable Energy Lab

Solar PV systems, wind turbine models, and hybrid energy storage systems.

Control & Automation Lab

PLC, SCADA, and industrial automation systems with Industry 4.0 technologies.
