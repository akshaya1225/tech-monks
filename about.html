Our Journey

History of BIT Bangalore

1979
Bangalore Institute of Technology was established with a vision to provide quality technical education. Started with just 3 engineering programs and 200 students.
1985
Expanded to 5 departments and introduced postgraduate programs. The institute gained recognition as one of the premier engineering colleges in Karnataka.
1995
Celebrated 15 years of excellence. Established research centers and collaborations with international universities.
2005
Received autonomous status from UGC, allowing curriculum flexibility and innovation in teaching methods.
2015
Ranked among the top 10 engineering colleges in Karnataka. Established Center of Excellence in AI, IoT, and Data Science.
2024
Celebrating 45 years of academic excellence with 10,000+ alumni worldwide, 50+ programs, and 200+ industry partners.

Today, Bangalore Institute of Technology stands as a beacon of quality education, having produced thousands of successful engineers, entrepreneurs, and leaders who have made significant contributions to society and industry globally.
