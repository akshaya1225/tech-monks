About the Department

The Department of Internet of Things (IoT) at Bangalore Institute of Technology is a pioneer in connected technologies education. Established in 2018, our department focuses on the intersection of hardware, software, and connectivity that powers the next generation of smart devices and intelligent systems.

With the world moving towards Industry 4.0 and smart cities, our program prepares students to design, develop, and deploy IoT solutions across various domains including healthcare, agriculture, manufacturing, and urban infrastructure. Our curriculum combines embedded systems, sensor networks, cloud computing, and data analytics to create complete IoT ecosystems.

Vision

To be a premier center for IoT education and research, nurturing innovators who will lead the connected world revolution and create intelligent solutions for global challenges.

Mission

To provide hands-on learning in embedded systems, sensor technologies, and cloud platforms; foster innovation through industry collaborations; and develop professionals capable of building end-to-end IoT solutions.

📚 Programs Offered

B.Tech in Internet of Things (IoT)

Duration: 4 Years | Intake: 60 Seats

Comprehensive program covering embedded systems, sensor networks, wireless communication, cloud integration, and IoT analytics.

M.Tech in IoT & Embedded Systems

Duration: 2 Years | Intake: 30 Seats

Advanced program focusing on industrial IoT, edge computing, real-time systems, and AI at the edge.

PG Diploma in Industrial IoT

Duration: 1 Year | Intake: 30 Seats

Industry-oriented program for working professionals focusing on Industry 4.0, predictive maintenance, and smart manufacturing.

📖 Curriculum Highlights

Year 1 - Foundation

  • Introduction to IoT & Embedded Systems
  • Programming in C & Python
  • Digital Electronics & Microcontrollers
  • Sensors & Actuators Fundamentals

Year 2 - Core IoT

  • Embedded Systems Design
  • Wireless Sensor Networks
  • IoT Communication Protocols (MQTT, CoAP)
  • Raspberry Pi & Arduino Programming

Year 3 - Advanced Topics

  • Cloud Computing for IoT (AWS IoT, Azure IoT)
  • Edge Computing & Fog Computing
  • Machine Learning for IoT
  • Real-time Operating Systems (RTOS)

Year 4 - Specialization & Project

  • Industrial IoT (IIoT) & Industry 4.0
  • Smart City Technologies
  • IoT Security & Privacy
  • Capstone IoT Project

🔬 Specialized IoT Labs

Embedded Systems Lab

State-of-the-art lab with ARM Cortex, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP32, and various microcontroller development boards.

Wireless Sensor Networks Lab

Complete setup for testing ZigBee, LoRa, NB-IoT, and 5G connectivity for IoT applications.

Cloud IoT Lab

Hands-on experience with AWS IoT Core, Azure IoT Hub, Google Cloud IoT, and IBM Watson IoT platforms.

Industry 4.0 Lab

Industrial automation setup with PLC, SCADA, robotic arms, and smart manufacturing demonstration.

IoT Analytics Lab

High-performance computing facility for real-time data processing, visualization, and predictive analytics.
