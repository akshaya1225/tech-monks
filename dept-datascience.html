About the Department

The Department of Data Science at Bangalore Institute of Technology is dedicated to training the next generation of data professionals who can extract actionable insights from complex data. Established in 2022, our department focuses on the intersection of statistics, computer science, and domain expertise.

With the explosion of big data across industries, our program equips students with skills in data mining, predictive modeling, business intelligence, and data visualization using industry-standard tools like Python, R, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI. Our graduates are sought after by top companies for their ability to turn raw data into strategic business decisions.

Vision

To be a premier data science education and research center, producing data leaders who drive data-driven decision-making across industries and create tangible value from data.

Mission

To provide rigorous training in statistical analysis and machine learning, foster research in emerging data science domains, and develop professionals who can transform complex data into strategic insights.

📚 Programs Offered

Duration: 4 Years | Intake: 60 Seats

M.Tech in Data Science & Analytics

Duration: 2 Years | Intake: 45 Seats

Advanced program focusing on big data technologies, predictive analytics, and business intelligence systems.

PG Diploma in Business Analytics

Duration: 1 Year | Intake: 30 Seats

Industry-focused program for working professionals seeking analytics expertise and career advancement.

📖 Curriculum Highlights

Year 1 - Foundation

  • Mathematics & Statistics for Data Science
  • Python Programming & Data Structures
  • Database Management Systems
  • Data Visualization Fundamentals

Year 2 - Core Data Science

  • Machine Learning Fundamentals
  • Data Mining & Warehousing
  • Big Data Technologies (Hadoop, Spark)
  • Statistical Learning & Inference

Year 3 - Advanced Topics

  • Deep Learning & Neural Networks
  • Time Series Analysis & Forecasting
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Cloud Data Engineering

Year 4 - Specialization & Project

  • MLOps & Production Machine Learning
  • Business Intelligence & Analytics
  • Industry Capstone Project
  • Research Internship
