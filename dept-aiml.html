About the Department

The Department of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at Bangalore Institute of Technology is at the forefront of intelligent systems education. Established in 2021, our department focuses on developing the next generation of AI researchers, data scientists, and ML engineers who will shape the future of technology.

With AI transforming every industry from healthcare to finance, our program provides a strong foundation in machine learning algorithms, deep learning architectures, natural language processing, and computer vision. Students work on real-world AI projects using industry-standard frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, and scikit-learn.

Vision

To be a global leader in AI education and research, producing innovators who leverage artificial intelligence to solve complex real-world problems and drive technological advancement.

Mission

To provide comprehensive AI education through industry-aligned curriculum, foster cutting-edge research in machine learning, and develop ethical AI practitioners who create responsible and impactful solutions.

Comprehensive program covering machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, NLP, and AI ethics.

Duration: 2 Years | Intake: 45 Seats

PG Diploma in Data Science & AI

Duration: 1 Year | Intake: 30 Seats

Industry-focused program for professionals seeking expertise in AI/ML applications.
